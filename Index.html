Introduction

In our system, we analyze three types of diseases-smallpox, polio and measles. And we use three kinds of graphs-choropleth,bar chart and line chart to present the data. In choropleth, users can select a specific disease to view its distribution among various states in diverse years. Here, we use different colors to encode the number of cases. And for the bar charts, we present the same type of data as choropleth here, but utilize diverse representational styles. In bar chart part, users can not only view the histograms ordering by the alphabet of states, but also sort by the number of cases. Finally, we did some simple data preprocessing in line charts. The data in the line chart is the total cases of the whole country in various years. The trends here can help the users have a better understanding of the inclination of a specific diseases.
