Abstract

Vaccination programs have led to dramatic declines in the incidence of contagious diseases in the United States over the past century. However, some of contagious diseases are still increasing in spite of the availability of vaccines. We use data visualization as a tool to help analyze the occurrence of contagious diseases in time-series.
